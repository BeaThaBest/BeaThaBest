AI solutions to large-scale enterprise clients across Türkiye.

Beyond my day job, I actively compete in hackathons (Meta Llama 2025 Finalist, KKB Agentic AI,

and take on freelance work to keep sharpening my craft on real-world problems.

I work across backend, frontend, mobile, AI integration, embedded systems, and DevOps —
comfortable moving between layers depending on what the problem needs.

Long-term: I want to launch my own product and build a company around it.

> production-deployed on Oracle Cloud.
>
> `React Native (Expo)` `Express.js` `TypeScript` `PostgreSQL` `Llama 3.2 70B`

---

### 🤖 PitchDrill — AI VC Council Platform
> **AImpact Hackathon 2025 · 48-hour build, 10 teams**
>
> Real-time pitch coaching platform where founders rehearse in front of a 5-character AI VC
> council. Built on Gemini Live Audio with PCM streaming, RAG over deck content via pgvector,
> WebSocket-driven event architecture.
>
> `Next.js 14` `FastAPI` `PostgreSQL + pgvector` `Gemini 3 Pro` `Gemini Live Audio`

---

>

> gazette with OCR/CAPTCHA solving) feed a 6-character AI credit committee that deliberates
> live over WebSocket + Redis Pub/Sub.
>
> `FastAPI` `React 18` `PostgreSQL` `Redis + Celery` `Qdrant` `Playwright` `Kubernetes`
